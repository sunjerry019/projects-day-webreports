Rationale

Inspiration & Objectives

3.575 million people die each year from water-related diseases, most people coming from undeveloped countries like Africa.

Because of this, we were inspired to find out more on getting rid of the bacteria in water.

The burden of several disease groups can only partly be attributed to water determinants. From this, we can see that water plays an essential role in the etiology of diseases.

We intend to find common food substances that are suspected or supposedly able to be anti- bacterial and make the water safer for consumption.
