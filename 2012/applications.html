3rd-World Countries

As mentioned earlier, we have created 1% agar and injected garlic extract, which demonstrated the highest efficacy against bacteria. This product can be inserted into the water filtration system of Singapore. This would allow the active compounds to dissasipitate into the water to kill the bacteria.

We have created 1% agar and injected garlic extract, which demonstrated the highest efficacy against bacteria. This anti-bacteria product can be used as an alternative to the comercially sold water cleansing pills. In the previous tests, our food extracts have proved to be heat-stable, being able to inhibit bacteria and protozoans despite being heated. People in countries who require water purification products can use our product to clean their water.

This anti-bacteria product can be inserted into the water, or even when the water is being boiled. The active compounds in the Garlic Extract could then dissipate out into the water, killing the bacteria in it.

This product could be distributed to poor families in third world countries without clean water to drink, where they could use it to clean water they use, which could improve their lives by making them healthier.
